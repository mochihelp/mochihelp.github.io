What is the reputation system?


As you create posts, vote, and comment on Mochi, you will gain reputation points. These exist to encourage users to post quality content and interact with the community.

You can see your current reputation score at the top left of the app and if you click it you will get more info about your current level and how far away you are from the next level.

There are currently 17 reputation levels each represented with their own emoji.

Verified users:


As you use Mochi you will see some users with a blue check mark under their emoji, which means the user is verified.

Verified users are those who have been determined by the dev to be wholesome, quality contributors to Mochi. These users are also normally reputable sources of help and information to do with the app.

Currently there is no concrete system of becoming verified and it is totally at the discretion of the developer of Mochi.

The toxicity filter:


Mochi uses a machine learning algorithm made by Google called perspective to keep the community and chats wholesome.

It is used in two places in the app: chats and posts. It is based only on the single message or post and it is not linked to the user.

There are known ways to bypass the filter such as using diacretics (accents above letters) if you feel it is unfairly stopping you from posting.

Bypassing the filter in order to post actually toxic posts will result in reports and potentially a device and account ban.

What does Mochi mean?

Mochi is named after a traditional japanese dessert of the same name, the mascot is also based on the dessert. Mochi ice cream is a small, round confection consisting of a soft, pounded sticky rice dumpling (mochi) formed around an ice cream filling. The ice cream flavors the confection while the mochi adds sweetness and texture. You can learn more about the dessert here.

Gifs on Mochi:


Mochi allows users to post externally hosted gifs. Gifs must be externally hosted because Mochi does not have the server capacity to store gifs due to the app not being monetized.

How to post gifs from giphy:

1: Open Giphy.com
2: On iPhone (on Safari) Tap and hold the Refresh button in the URL bar then tap Request Desktop Site.
2: On android (on Chrome) Tap the three dots in the top right corner of the screen and then tap Desktop Site
3: Now search for your gif and tap on the one you like
4: Then tap the Media button to the left of the gif and copy either the Social or Small link
5: Open Mochi, click the media button, and select gif
6: Paste in your gif link and click add gif
7: Wait for Mochi to finish checking your gif before you hit post, otherwise your gif will not post successfully
